# How to Add Your Profile Image

## Step 1: Save Your Image
1. Save your profile photo as **profile.jpg** (recommended: 500x500px minimum)
2. Place it in the **public** folder of your project:
   - Path: `D:\Nextjs\my_portfolio\public\profile.jpg`

## Step 2: Optimize Your Image (Recommended)
For better performance and SEO, optimize your image using an online tool:
- Visit: https://tinypng.com/ (free, works for both PNG and JPG)
- Upload your image
- Download the compressed version
- Save it as `profile.jpg` in the public folder

## Step 3: Verify
After saving, your portfolio will automatically display the image in the Hero section.

## Common Image Issues & Solutions
- **Image too large?** Compress it with TinyPNG
- **Wrong aspect ratio?** Crop to square (1:1 ratio)
- **Image not showing?** Clear browser cache (Ctrl + Shift + Delete)
